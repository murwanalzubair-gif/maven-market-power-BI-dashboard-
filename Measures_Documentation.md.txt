# DAX Measures Documentation

All measures are stored in a dedicated `_Measures` table and written in dependency order to avoid calculation errors.

---

### 1. Total Revenue
Total Revenue = SUMX(Fact_Transactions, Fact_Transactions[quantity] * RELATED(Dim_Products[product_retail_price]))

**Description:** Sum of revenue generated from all transactions (quantity × retail price per line item).

---

### 2. Total Cost
Total Cost = SUMX(Fact_Transactions, Fact_Transactions[quantity] * RELATED(Dim_Products[product_cost]))

**Description:** Sum of the cost of goods sold across all transactions (quantity × product cost per line item).

---

### 3. Total Profit
Total Profit = [Total Revenue] - [Total Cost]

**Description:** Gross profit generated. Depends on Total Revenue and Total Cost being defined first.

---

### 4. Profit Margin %
Profit Margin % = DIVIDE([Total Profit], [Total Revenue], 0)

**Description:** Profit as a percentage of revenue. Uses DIVIDE to safely handle division by zero.

---

### 5. Transactions Count
Transactions Count = COUNTROWS(Fact_Transactions)

**Description:** Total number of transaction line items.

---

### 6. Returns Count
Returns Count = COUNTROWS(Fact_Returns)

**Description:** Total number of return line items.

---

### 7. Quantity Sold
Quantity Sold = SUM(Fact_Transactions[quantity])

**Description:** Total number of units sold.

---

### 8. Quantity Returned
Quantity Returned = SUM(Fact_Returns[quantity])

**Description:** Total number of units returned.

---

### 9. Return Rate %
Return Rate % = DIVIDE([Returns Count], [Transactions Count], 0)

**Description:** Proportion of transactions that resulted in a return. Uses DIVIDE to safely handle division by zero.

---

## Dependency Order Summary
Total Revenue and Total Cost are needed before Total Profit, which is needed before Profit Margin %.
Transactions Count and Returns Count are needed before Return Rate %.

Note: Measures referencing other measures (e.g., Total Profit, Profit Margin %, Return Rate %) must be created after their dependencies to avoid reference errors in Power BI.
